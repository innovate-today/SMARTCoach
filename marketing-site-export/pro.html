What Pro includes.

Pro is for programs that need synced team data, not just a stopwatch.

SMARTCoach Pro Mobile App

Use the phone at practice and meets to capture timing and results where the work happens.

  • Training and meet groups
  • Start, lap, finish, bulk timing
  • Rep/rest splits and target review
  • Relay timing with leg selectors
  • Field-event result capture
  • Archive and bulk archive workflows

SMART Trak Sync

Send completed workouts and meet results from the phone into the coach dashboard.

  • Planned workout context
  • Volume and completed work review
  • Weather, surface, and notes capture
  • PB/SB result flags where supported
  • Corrections and void handling
  • Signed coach access sessions

Team Limits

Pro pricing follows active team size, and archived athletes do not count against the active limit.

  • Pro 25: up to 25 active athletes
  • Pro 100: up to 100 active athletes
  • Pro 200: up to 200 active athletes
  • Pro Unlimited: custom/manual offer for unlimited active athletes
  • Shared coach code plus up to 10 assistant coach seats
  • One code can unlock desktop and phone workflow

How Pro fits the season.

Start by adding athletes and training groups. Set current fitness marks so targets can be calculated. Build, upload, or manually create workouts. Then use the phone app at practice or meets and sync the completed work back to SMART Trak.

  • Training Calendar handles daily coaching work
  • Upload/Paste Plan handles full-plan imports
  • Auto Build Plan drafts plans for coach review
  • Meet History and Dashboard organize results after race day
