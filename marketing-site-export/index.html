Current product direction

Training Calendar is the daily coaching hub.

Coaches use the Training Calendar for one-day workouts, races, rest or off days, notes, status updates, mileage logging, race results, and meet management. Upload/Paste Plan is for full-plan upload or paste workflows, and Auto Build Plan creates a coach-reviewed draft plan.

  • Drag workouts and meets to another day
  • Bulk update scheduled, completed, or skipped statuses
  • Log miles and race results from the calendar workflow
  • Connect phone-app timing back to planned training days

Features across mobile and desktop.

The site includes every current product area that matters for sales conversations.

Stopwatch

Training groups, meet groups, archive, starts, laps, finishes, rep/rest splits, target chips, runner reordering, relays, field-event capture, and sync.

Roster

Active athletes, groups, athlete details, gender-aware comparisons, parent contact storage, current fitness setup, and athlete profile context.

Training

Training Calendar, Upload/Paste Plan, Athlete Setup, Auto Build Plan, plan assignments, current fitness marks, mileage, and completed workout review.

Meets

Meet History, Manage Meets, Log Race Result, relays, field marks, PB/SB flags, meet comparison, school records, Track Simulator, and XC Simulator.
