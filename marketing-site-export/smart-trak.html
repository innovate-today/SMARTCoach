SMART Trak

SMART Trak is the desktop workspace where coaches manage the season: roster, training calendar, plans, mileage, race results, meet history, school records, weather, and team scoring simulators.

Get SMART Trak with ProView product tour

Every SMART Trak page.

These are the current desktop tools coaches can use in SMARTCoach Pro.

Dashboard

Roster overview, training load, volume miles, recent meet results, completed workouts, filters, exports, Staff Access, Log Miles, Log Race Result, and Manage Meets.

Athletes

Coach-facing roster, active/inactive status, groups, athlete details, gender display, parent contact fields, notes, and parent contact organization.

Training Calendar

Week and list views, workouts, races, rest days, notes, drag-to-reschedule, bulk status updates, mileage, race results, and meet management.

Athlete Setup

Current fitness marks, girls/boys/unlisted filters, training groups, plan assignments, and group roster setup shared with the phone app.

Upload/Paste Plan

Full-plan upload or paste workflows for coaches bringing existing training plans into SMART Trak.

Auto Build Plan

Guided plan drafting for a chosen period, with coach review and adjustment before use.

Meet History

Meet schedule, meet result comparison, event/gender-aware fastest and rank comparisons, relay details, PB/SB context, and correction support.

Records

School record board with manual add, paste, template upload, edit/delete, current and historical status, and boys/girls record separation.

Track Simulator

Track meet scoring using saved opponent fields, SMART Trak season bests, scoring rules, exclusions, and copyable output.

XC Simulator

Cross country scoring simulator using saved opponents, season-best sources, and hypothetical meet outcomes.

Weather

Live weather without an API key, saved locations, current conditions, hourly forecast, daily forecast, and city/state search.

Athlete Calendar

Athlete-facing calendar portal where assigned athletes can mark workouts complete, modified, or skipped.

Designed for clean coach data.

  • Avoids coach-facing CRM wording
  • Uses SMART Trak labels and familiar coach workflows
  • Separates school record board updates from everyday meet result entry
  • Supports signed coach sessions and code changes
  • Works inside a custom sidebar iframe when embedded
