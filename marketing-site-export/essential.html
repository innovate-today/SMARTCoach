What Essential is for.

Essential is the simplest subscription: the SMARTCoach mobile stopwatch, active-code protected, with one active device session at a time.

Timing

Use the mobile app for practices and meets with training groups, meet groups, starts, laps, finishes, runner ordering, and shareable timing output.

Simple Access

Essential requires an active code. A new Essential login displaces the previous active device session, keeping the subscription tied to one active device workflow.

Upgrade Path

When a team needs rosters, calendars, synced workouts, meet history, records, and desktop reporting, upgrade to SMARTCoach Pro.

Essential keeps the stopwatch focused.

  • Training, Meets, and Archive sections
  • Group setup and runner management
  • Start, Lap, Finish, Reset, Sort, Sum, Add, Settings, and Delete controls
  • Useful for coaches who are not ready for a desktop team system

Essential does not include SMART Trak pages, SMART Trak sync, desktop dashboard, roster management, training calendar, records, or simulators.

Need more than stopwatch timing?

SMARTCoach Pro adds SMART Trak, active-athlete tiers, a shared coach code with assistant coach seats, training calendars, meet history, records, and scoring simulators.
