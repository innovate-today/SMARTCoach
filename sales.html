Coach-facing tools without a data mess.

SMART Trak is built around the real season rhythm: update the roster, set fitness, build or import plans, log mileage, capture race results, and keep records separate from everyday meet entry.

  • Training Calendar is the home for one-day workouts, races, rest/off days, and notes.
  • Upload/Paste Plan handles full-plan upload and paste workflows.
  • Auto Build Plan creates a draft plan for coach review and adjustment.
  • Track and XC simulators use saved fields and SMART Trak season bests.

Account setup is built for SaaS rollout.

Purchases provision the customer account first. Support completes the SMART Trak connection, saves the shared coach code, and validates the account before the coach invite goes out. Beta customers can begin on a 30-day Pro 100 trial.

  • GHL SaaS provisions the sub-account and applies the SMARTCoach snapshot.
  • The account custom value powers the SMART Trak custom menu link.
  • Recurring subscription updates send billing/access fields only.
  • Private integration tokens and coach codes stay out of billing payloads.
  • GHL sends trialing during the 30-day beta trial, then active or blocked billing status after payment.
  • Lower-plan downgrades require the active athlete count to fit the requested plan first.
What coaches should hear:

SMARTCoach Pro gives your staff one shared coach-code workflow for desktop SMART Trak and the mobile app. Keep the code limited to active staff so synced team data stays clean.
